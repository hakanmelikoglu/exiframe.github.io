Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2024

1. Information We Collect

EXIFrame does not collect any personal information. We respect your privacy and operate with a local-first approach:

  • All photo processing is done locally on your device
  • EXIF data is read and processed entirely on-device
  • No data is uploaded to external servers

2. Photo Access

EXIFrame requires access to your photo library to:

  • Read photos you choose to edit
  • Save processed photos back to your library
  • Access EXIF metadata from your photos

We never access your photos without your explicit permission through iOS system dialogs.

3. Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device, including:

  • App preferences
  • Custom frame settings
  • Temporary processing files

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Apple's App Store for app distribution and purchases
  • StoreKit for in-app purchases
